# BrewNectar Copy Rewrite Brief

## Summary
Elite direct response copywriting rewrite of the entire landing page using principles from:
- **Breakthrough Advertising** (Eugene Schwartz) — unique mechanism, mass desire, big idea, awareness-level headline
- **Scientific Advertising** (Claude Hopkins) — reason-why copy, specificity, write to one person, proof first

## Target Audience
Cold Meta traffic: knowledge workers, founders, students, creatives. Problem-aware, solution-curious, skeptical. Already drink coffee daily.

## Key Rewrite Sections
1. **Hero/Headline** — Replace command with tension/curiosity hook. Lead with the gap they feel.
2. **Problem Section** — Rewrite from list to empathetic narrative with escalation.
3. **Ingredient/Science** — Lead with human benefit, then science as proof.
4. **How It Works** — Tighten micro-copy, make it vivid payoff not assembly instructions.
5. **Social Proof** — Make testimonials specific, persona-segmented, objection-answering.
6. **Offer/Pricing** — Justify price with cost-of-inaction framing. Prominent risk reversal.
7. **CTA Buttons** — Desire-forward ("Give Me Calm Focus") not action-forward ("Shop Now").
8. **FAQ** — Each answer sells + answers concern. Soft push at end.

## Tone Rules
- Direct, confident, never hype-y
- Short sentences. White space. Momentum.
- No buzzwords, no passive voice
- Knowledgeable friend who knows neuroscience

## Constraints
- No "journey", no "Are you...", no decorative em-dashes
- No padding — every sentence must pull weight
- Keep page structure the same, only change words
