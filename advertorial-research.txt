ADVERTORIAL RESEARCH NOTES
==========================

SERENE HERBS (sereneherbs.com/pages/flush-out-the-toxics)
- Layout: Two-column — content on left, sticky product card on right
- Sticky sidebar: Product image + bullet benefits + "CHECK AVAILABILITY" CTA + guarantee badge
- Multiple CTAs throughout: "CHECK AVAILABILITY" buttons after key sections
- Section structure: What is it → How fast → Testimonials → How it works → Weight loss → Parasites → How to take → Safety → Pricing → Where to buy → Guarantee → Final CTA
- Uses urgency: countdown timer at bottom
- Author byline with photo for credibility
- Image carousel at top
- Weakness: Design is very basic/template-looking, green color scheme feels cheap
- Strength: Sticky sidebar CTA is always visible, multiple CTAs throughout

TRYTANOVA (trytanova.com/pages/listacle)
- Layout: Numbered listicle format "7 Reasons Why..."
- Full-width hero with title, subtitle, author byline, date
- "As Seen In" press logos bar (Bazaar, Elle, Cosmopolitan, Vogue, Allure)
- Alternating image/text layout for each numbered reason
- CTA buttons after key sections: "Try Tanova Today", "Shop Now"
- Testimonial carousel with real photos, names, ages, locations
- Ingredient breakdown section
- Final urgency CTA: "Start Your Daily Glow" 
- No sticky nav or sidebar — just inline CTAs
- Weakness: No sticky navigation, no way to jump between sections
- Strength: Numbered listicle format is scannable, good testimonial carousel

DRIVSE (shower.drivse.com/list2108)
- Layout: Numbered listicle "9 Reasons" format
- Fake publication header "HOME-INSIDER" for credibility
- Urgency banner at top with countdown timer + sale messaging
- Author byline with photo + "Verified" badge
- Alternating image/text for each numbered point
- Video testimonials inline
- Single CTA at bottom: "Get 50% OFF"
- Clean, editorial design — feels like reading a magazine article
- Weakness: Only one CTA at bottom, no sticky nav
- Strength: Editorial credibility feel, clean typography, numbered format is scannable

SYNTHESIS — BEST PRACTICES FOR BREWNECTAR ADVERTORIAL:
1. Sticky section navigation (horizontal pill bar) — jump to any section
2. Multiple CTAs after every 2-3 sections — "Get BrewNectar" or "Check Availability"
3. Numbered listicle format for scannability
4. Author byline with credibility markers
5. Alternating image/text layout
6. Testimonial section with real-feeling quotes
7. Ingredient science section
8. "As Seen In" or credibility markers
9. Guarantee callout
10. Final urgency CTA
11. Match BrewNectar's warm cream aesthetic
